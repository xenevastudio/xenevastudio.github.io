Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2024-01-01

XenEva Studio (“we”, “us”, or “our”) develops and publishes games on the Google Play Store. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our games and website.

Information We Collect

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers for analytics and security.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics data (such as session length, in-game actions, and crash reports) to improve our games.
  • Personal Information: We do not collect personal information (such as name, email, or address) unless you provide it voluntarily (e.g., via contact form or support request).

How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve our games and services
  • To analyze usage and fix bugs or crashes
  • To respond to user support requests

Third-Party Services

Our games may use third-party services (such as Google Play Services, AdMob, or analytics providers) that may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Your Rights

  • You may contact us to request deletion of your data or to ask questions about our privacy practices.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at contact.xeneva@gmail.com.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Please review it periodically for changes.
